About Us

Welcome to The Yaady academy,where we believe in nurturing young minds with quality Islamic education. Our online madrassah is tailored for children aged 5 to 16, providing a supportive and engaging environment for learning.

Learn with an experienced, well trained Quran tutor with over 15 years of teaching the Quran for both male and female of all ages in Yaady Academy. We are well known for our quality Quran online courses because they are exciting, effective, and transmit the love of Quran to you.

Our History

Sheikh Yasir Abdul-hafeez Abdullah (Al-Adabby) is an erudite Islamic scholar from Ogun-State. He is an Alumni from the Arabic School of Fadeelat-us-sheikh Kamaldeen Al-Adabby in Ilorin under the tutelage of Sheikh Yakub Ali-Agan. After completion of his (Sanowiy) Secondary Education from Ilorin, he won a scholarship to continue his postgraduate studies from Al-Azhar University Cairo, Egypt.

He returned to Nigeria after spending fruitful years in Egypt to impact tremendously in his community and the state at large. He heads many Islamic association in Ogun-state including NAJOMO, AL-HAYAT, MIUN, QUAREEB, ADABIYAH etc. Sheikh Yasir is a prolific teacher, mentor, preacher who has been frequenting Saudi Arabia for Hajj rites for over 20 years. He retired as a Vice Principal from a Secondary School.
